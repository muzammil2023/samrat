ABOUT BRIDGESTONE TYRE :

Bridgestone Group with its headquarters in Tokyo is the world's largest tyre manufacturing company. Bridgestone globally has more than 100 production facilities. Around the world top fleets and Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEM) rely on Bridgestone tyres for high mileage and low cost per kilometer.

Bridgestone has marked its presence in India for more than 20 years now. Bridgestone has set up its manufacturing facility for Truck & Bus radial tyres in Chakan, Pune.

The Indian transportation industry is one of the significant contributors to the India's GDP. This industry is evolving at a rapid pace with recent government initiatives like infrastructure development and new environmental regulations.

At the same time, Indian Fleet owners are continuously struggling to meet the expectations of the ever-demanding consumers and to achieve operational excellence.

Tyres are one of the most important components to fleet's operating cost. Every fleet owner basically expects high tyre mileage, better fuel savings, strong casing, better retreads, and so on.

Considering all these diverse requirements, Bridgestone India provides a wide range of commercial tyres solutions. Our truck and bus radial tyres are designed with latest technology which can help lowering fleet operating expenses.
