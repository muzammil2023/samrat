ABOUT YOKOHAMA TYRE ::

The Yokohama Rubber Company, Limited is a tire company based in Tokyo, Japan. The company was founded and began on October 13, 1917, in a joint venture between Yokohama Cable Manufacturing and B.F. Goodrich. In 1969, the company expanded to the United States as Yokohama Tyre Corporation.
