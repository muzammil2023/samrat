ABOUT CONTINENTAL TYRE ::

Tires are the essential point of contact between a vehicle and the road. We work to ensure that your tires will always offer excellent braking, maximum safety, and pure driving pleasure. That's why Continental is one of the leading manufacturers of tires in Europe.We have been making tires for more than 150 years: big ones and small ones, heavy ones and thin ones, fast ones and strong ones. Or tires have been on race cars and Tour de France bikes, they have broken speed and range records, they are regularly tested best in class and have often been the first of their kind.
