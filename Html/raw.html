ABOUT US

We Samrat Tyre located in Rajkot, Gujarat, Saurashtra are home to premium tyre brands for passengers' different varieties of vehicles. we have earned a reputation in the markets for providing fair pricing. best quality product and excellent services to our customers. More than just being a place where a customer can buy a tyre, we aim to give all our customers an international level of service at domestic prices.

We welcome you to the Samrat tyre. we introduce ourselves as a leading retailer in all segments of tyre. Our journey started with our grandfather Ram Bahadur Murav as a re-trader and remold company by the name of Rajesh tyre in 1963. we were in business gst tyre remold. and many more government projects. we also participated in a government and private auction for an old tyre by the name of Samrat Scrap in the year 2003 by Naresh Murav. Our leading companies were RMC, PGVCL, ESSAR, and many more. We have that experience and with the blessing of our grandfather, we entered the new tyre retail business by the name of Samrat tyre in the year 2010 by Rajesh Murav with the innovative tyre. We had developed our tyre by the name eco sprinter by innovative tyre. We were the leading distributor till 2020. We started branded company dealerships like Birla, Apollo, and Continental in 2014. We started Vinayak tyre by Vijay Murav as an exclusive Apollo tyre dealer to date. we had even supplying tyre to the government like Airport Authorities and many more government companies. we entered in car tyre business by the name of Samrat Enterprise lead by Ajay Murav in year 2021 with the dealership in Continental and Bridgestone with all the experience of our journey of over 60 years in this field and we believe and providing almost service to our customers.
